#### **Option 2: The "Day Trip" (Serravalle)**

* **Where:** **Serravalle Designer Outlet**.
* **Why:** This is the **largest designer outlet in Europe**. It is a destination in itself.

* **Plan:** Drive straight to Milan on the 13th. On the 14th or 15th, take a dedicated day trip (1 hour drive or shuttle bus) south to Serravalle.
